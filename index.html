About Lightbox LED

Dedicated to empowering businesses with professional photography solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Lightbox LED emerged from a simple observation: small businesses and entrepreneurs were struggling to compete with large corporations when it came to product photography. We saw talented creators spending thousands on professional photographers or investing in expensive, complicated equipment they didn't know how to use. We decided to change that. Our mission was to democratize professional-quality product photography by creating affordable, easy-to-use lighting solutions that anyone could master in minutes. Today, thousands of businesses worldwide trust our kits to showcase their products and drive sales.

Our Mission & Values

We believe that every business deserves access to professional-quality tools and resources. Our mission is to empower entrepreneurs, small business owners, and content creators with innovative lighting solutions that eliminate barriers to professional photography. We're committed to continuous innovation, exceptional customer service, and creating products that deliver real, measurable results. Quality, accessibility, and customer success are at the heart of everything we do. We don't just sell lighting kits – we partner with our customers to help them grow their businesses and achieve their dreams through the power of great visual storytelling.

Our complete Lightbox LED Photography Kit includes:

  • Two professional-grade LED light panels with adjustable brightness and color temperature
  • Two sturdy light stands with smooth height adjustment (3-7 feet)
  • One premium 24" x 36" photography backdrop with multiple color options (white, black, and gray)
  • Complete backdrop support system with clamps and crossbar
  • Professional-quality diffusers to soften and even out light
  • Comprehensive setup guide and video tutorials
  • 24/7 customer support access

Everything you need to start taking professional product photos is included – no additional purchases required.

Our kit is designed for incredibly fast setup:

  • Unboxing & Inspection: 2 minutes
  • Assembling Light Stands: 2 minutes per stand
  • Attaching LED Panels: 1 minute per panel
  • Setting Up Backdrop: 3-5 minutes
  • Positioning & Adjusting: 2-3 minutes

Total setup time is typically 10-15 minutes. Once positioned, you can start taking professional photos immediately. The LED lights are plug-and-play with simple controls for brightness and color temperature adjustment.
